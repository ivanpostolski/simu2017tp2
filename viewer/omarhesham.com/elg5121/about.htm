About Us

Team members: Mohammad El-Shabani (University of Ottawa) and Omar Hesham (Carleton University)

Both members worked on all aspects of the project, with Mohammad leading the programming tutorial, and Omar leading the video section.

We're a group in Prof. Abdulmotaleb El Saddik's ELG5121 Multimedia Communications class. The term project involved developing a short course on a multimedia protocol or concpet that hadn't been taught yet in class.
Both parts of our short course (conceptual videos and practical programming) were developed for academic non-commercial purposes only. All rights reserved unless otherwise indicated.
